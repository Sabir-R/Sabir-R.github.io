My Passion

I’m a recent graduate of the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, holding a B.S. in Information Science and a minor in Computer Science. I’m passionate about using data to drive insight and innovation, particularly by applying Machine Learning and AI to solve real-world business problems.

Through both academic projects and hands-on experience, I’ve worked with large datasets, built SQL databases, deployed websites using GCP, and implemented predictive models with Python. My technical background is complemented by leadership experience in both technical support and e-sports operations, where I’ve led teams, managed training programs, and coordinated events for hundreds of participants.

I’m especially interested in opportunities that combine data science, problem-solving, and technology-driven decision-making.
